# 📊 End-to-End E-Commerce Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to uncover insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product trends, and regional performance.
The objective is to transform raw business data into actionable insights that can support strategic decision-making
## Business Questions
1. Which product categories generate the highest sales?
2. Which categories are the most profitable?
3. Which regions contribute the most revenue?
4. How do sales trends change over time?
5. Which products generate the highest profit?
6. Which products generate losses?
7. Who are the top customers by sales?
8. How do discounts impact profitability?

## Key Findings
- Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
- West region contributed the highest revenue.
- Sales showed an overall increasing trend over time.
- Several technology products generated significant profits.
- Some products consistently generated losses.
- A small group of customers contributed a large share of total sales.
- Higher discounts were associated with lower profitability.
## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-profit products.
- Reduce excessive discounting.
- Improve performance in low-performing regions.
- Retain high-value customers through loyalty programs.
- Review loss-making products and pricing strategies.
